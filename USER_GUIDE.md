# Tres3 Video Chat - User Guide
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** October 19, 2025

## 🎥 In-Call Features

### Video Controls

#### Picture-in-Picture (PiP)
- **Tap** the small video window → Resize between small and large
- **Long press** the small video window → Swap main and PiP videos
  - Main video becomes PiP
  - PiP becomes main video
  - Great for focusing on different participants!

#### Camera & Microphone
- **Mic Button** (bottom center-left)
  - Tap to mute/unmute your microphone
  - Red = muted, White = active
  
- **Camera Switch** (bottom center-right)
  - Tap to toggle your camera on/off
  - Flip between front and rear cameras

### Call Controls

#### End Call
- **Red Phone Button** (bottom center)
  - Instantly ends the call for you
  - Other participants remain connected

#### Add Person to Call
- **Person+ Button** (bottom right)
  1. Tap to open the invite dialog
  2. Enter the person's email address
  3. Tap "Invite" to send the invitation
  4. They'll receive a notification to join!

### Advanced Features

#### Menu (⋮ button, bottom left)

**Participants**
- See everyone in the call
- View their mic/camera status
- Check who's active or muted
- Shows participant count

**Share Screen**
- Share your screen with others
- Button turns green when sharing
- Tap again to stop sharing
- Perfect for presentations!

### Notifications

#### Incoming Call
When someone calls you:
- Full-screen notification appears
- Shows caller's name
- Two options:
  - **Accept** (green button) → Join the call
  - **Decline** (red button) → Reject and notify caller

#### Call Invitations
When invited to join an ongoing call:
- Notification shows who invited you
- Tap to join the active call
- See all participants already in the call

## ⚡ Quick Tips

### For Best Call Quality
1. ✅ Use a stable Wi-Fi connection
2. ✅ Ensure good lighting for your camera
3. ✅ Use headphones to prevent echo
4. ✅ Close other apps to free up resources

### Gestures & Shortcuts
- **Single tap background** → Show/hide controls
- **Tap PiP video** → Resize PiP window
- **Long press PiP** → Swap video feeds
- **Tap outside dialogs** → Close them

### Privacy & Security
- 🔒 All calls are end-to-end encrypted (LiveKit)
- 🔒 Only invited participants can join
- 🔒 Screen sharing requires explicit permission
- 🔒 Your camera/mic can be disabled anytime

## 📱 Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Starting a Call
1. Open Tres3 app
2. Select a contact from your list
3. Tap to initiate call
4. Wait for them to accept
5. Call begins when they join!

### Scenario 2: Joining an Ongoing Call
1. Receive invitation notification
2. Tap to accept
3. See all current participants
4. Your video/audio joins automatically

### Scenario 3: Screen Sharing
1. Join or start a call
2. Tap menu (⋮) button
3. Select "Share Screen"
4. Grant permission (first time only)
5. Your screen is now visible to others
6. Tap "Stop Sharing" when done

### Scenario 4: Managing Multiple Participants
1. Start a 1-on-1 call
2. Tap "Add Person" (👤+)
3. Enter participant's email
4. Tap "Invite"
5. They receive notification
6. Call expands to group call!

### Scenario 5: Declining a Call
1. Receive incoming call notification
2. Tap "Decline" button
3. Caller is notified you declined
4. Notification disappears

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Can't see other person's video"
- ✅ Check their camera is enabled
- ✅ Verify your internet connection
- ✅ Try toggling your own camera off/on
- ✅ Check participants list for their status

### "Other person can't hear me"
- ✅ Check mic isn't muted (should be white, not red)
- ✅ Check phone isn't on silent mode
- ✅ Grant microphone permission if prompted
- ✅ Try toggling mic off/on

### "Controls disappeared"
- ✅ Just tap anywhere on the screen
- ✅ They auto-hide after a few seconds
- ✅ This is normal behavior!

### "Can't add person to call"
- ✅ Verify email address is correct
- ✅ Ensure they have a Tres3 account
- ✅ Check your internet connection
- ✅ Try again in a moment

### "Screen sharing not working"
- ✅ Grant screen capture permission
- ✅ Close other apps using screen recording
- ✅ Restart the call if needed
- ✅ Check if feature is supported on your device

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Better Video Quality**
   - Position yourself in well-lit area
   - Keep camera lens clean
   - Use rear camera for better quality

2. **Privacy During Calls**
   - Use virtual background (if supported)
   - Position camera to avoid showing private areas
   - Remember to disable screen sharing when done

3. **Smooth Group Calls**
   - Invite participants one at a time
   - Use participants list to monitor who's connected
   - Mute when not speaking in large groups

4. **Network Issues?**
   - Switch to audio-only (disable camera)
   - Reduces bandwidth usage
   - Maintains call quality
